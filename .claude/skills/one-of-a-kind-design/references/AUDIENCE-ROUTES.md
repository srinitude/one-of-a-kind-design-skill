## Routes by Industry

### Fintech / Banking
- **Primary Styles:** material-design, dark-mode-ui, bento-ui, swiss-international
- **Avoid:** psychedelic, memphis-design, glitch, pixel-art
- **Rationale:** Trust, clarity, and data density are non-negotiable. Avoid anything that signals imprecision or play.

### Luxury / Fashion
- **Primary Styles:** art-deco, cinematic, minimalist-maximalism, editorial-minimalism
- **Avoid:** flat-design, material-design, pixel-art, claymorphism
- **Rationale:** Premium perception requires editorial restraint or dramatic excess -- never default SaaS patterns.

### Children / Education
- **Primary Styles:** claymorphism, papercut, isometric, tactile-craft-digital
- **Avoid:** brutalist-web, constructivism, dark-mode-ui, deconstructivism
- **Rationale:** Approachability, warmth, and clarity. Avoid anything confrontational or spatially disorienting.

### Developer Tools
- **Primary Styles:** dark-mode-ui, wireframe-mesh, bento-ui, flat-design
- **Avoid:** rococo, art-nouveau, psychedelic, impressionism
- **Rationale:** Information density, monospace typography, and reduced visual noise. Serif and ornament are alien.

### Sustainability / Climate
- **Primary Styles:** solarpunk, wabi-sabi, scandinavian-minimalism, resonant-stark
- **Avoid:** y2k-revival, vaporwave, pop-art, hdr-hyperrealism
- **Rationale:** Material honesty and nature connection. Avoid synthetic, nostalgic, or hyper-processed aesthetics.

### Music / Entertainment
- **Primary Styles:** psychedelic, vaporwave, glitch, afrofuturism, cinematic
- **Avoid:** swiss-international, material-design, editorial-minimalism
- **Rationale:** Expressive, immersive, personality-driven. Rational/systematic styles suppress creative energy.

### Healthcare / Wellness
- **Primary Styles:** scandinavian-minimalism, resonant-stark, wabi-sabi, flat-design
- **Avoid:** glitch, brutalist-web, constructivism, psychedelic
- **Rationale:** Calm, trustworthy, accessible. Avoid visual aggression, disorientation, or motion-heavy patterns.

### Creator Economy / Indie SaaS
- **Primary Styles:** neubrutalism, bento-ui, editorial-minimalism, pop-art
- **Avoid:** rococo, art-deco, skeuomorphism, hdr-hyperrealism
- **Rationale:** Personality and differentiation over polish. Show character, not luxury.

## Routes by Market Segment (from implementation_intelligence.audience_router)

### B2B SaaS / Enterprise
- **Safe Defaults:** flat-design, material-design, swiss-international
- **Break the Mold:**
  - `art-deco` -- Geometric rigor reads as premium authority; your enterprise tool stops looking like every other gray dashboard
  - `brutalist-web` -- For developer-facing products, raw structure signals technical credibility over marketing polish
- **Actively Avoid:**
  - `psychedelic` -- Decision-making contexts need clarity, not sensory overload
  - `surrealism` -- Spatial disorientation conflicts with productivity goals

### Consumer Fintech
- **Safe Defaults:** flat-design, dark-mode-ui, scandinavian-minimalism
- **Break the Mold:**
  - `claymorphism` -- The tactile softness reduces financial anxiety; money feels less intimidating when the interface feels friendly
  - `art-deco` -- Signals 'old money' confidence; the opposite of fintech's typical startup-blue aesthetic
- **Actively Avoid:**
  - `glitch` -- Data corruption aesthetics in a financial context signals unreliability
  - `brutalist-web` -- Raw structure in a money context reads as 'we didn't invest in security'

### Healthcare / Wellness
- **Safe Defaults:** scandinavian-minimalism, flat-design
- **Break the Mold:**
  - `wabi-sabi` -- Imperfection-as-beauty directly serves mental health messaging; the aesthetic IS the therapy
  - `aurora-ui` -- Atmospheric gradients create calming environments that reduce clinical anxiety
  - `impressionism` -- The soft luminosity creates a non-institutional feeling that clinical UIs desperately need
- **Actively Avoid:**
  - `constructivism` -- The aggressive energy and high contrast create anxiety in health contexts
  - `memphis-design` -- Clashing colors and chaotic patterns conflict with the calm needed in health decisions

### Creative Tools / Design Software
- **Safe Defaults:** dark-mode-ui, flat-design, material-design
- **Break the Mold:**
  - `swiss-international` -- The tool's UI should model the design discipline it enables; Swiss grid for a design tool is meta-competence
  - `constructivism` -- The energetic asymmetry signals creative freedom; Linear uses constructivist energy effectively
- **Actively Avoid:**
  - `rococo` -- Ornamental excess in a tool context competes with the user's creative output
  - `skeuomorphism` -- Realistic textures date fast and create visual competition with the user's canvas

### E-commerce / DTC
- **Safe Defaults:** studio-product, scandinavian-minimalism
- **Break the Mold:**
  - `risograph` -- The analog texture differentiates instantly from the endless white-background product grid
  - `ukiyo-e` -- For culturally-rooted products, the woodblock aesthetic creates narrative depth that lifestyle photography cannot
- **Actively Avoid:**
  - `wireframe-mesh` -- The technical skeleton aesthetic works against the desire to present finished, desirable products
  - `cellular-automata` -- The binary-grid abstraction removes all product warmth

### Education / EdTech
- **Safe Defaults:** material-design, flat-design, claymorphism
- **Break the Mold:**
  - `bauhaus` -- The shapes-as-primitives visual language is pre-literate-friendly and inherently pedagogical
  - `isometric` -- Dimensional illustration explains spatial concepts that flat diagrams cannot
  - `papercut` -- The layered, tactile quality connects digital learning to physical craft
- **Actively Avoid:**
  - `surrealism` -- Disorienting spatial relationships conflict with learning clarity
  - `brutalist-web` -- Raw exposure of structure intimidates novice users who need guardrails

### Music / Audio / Entertainment
- **Safe Defaults:** dark-mode-ui, aurora-ui
- **Break the Mold:**
  - `vaporwave` -- For artists who want nostalgic-digital identity, nothing else carries the same cultural weight
  - `skeuomorphism` -- Audio production interfaces are the ONE context where realistic knobs and sliders are not just acceptable but expected
  - `afrofuturism` -- For artists in Afro-diasporic music traditions, the style carries cultural meaning that generic dark-mode cannot
- **Actively Avoid:**
  - `swiss-international` -- The objective rationality conflicts with music's emotional expression
  - `studio-product` -- The clinical white-background approach removes all atmosphere from what should be an immersive experience

## Routes by Audience Age

### Gen Z (18-28)
- **Resonant Styles:** y2k-revival, neubrutalism, vaporwave, claymorphism, pixel-art
- **Anti-Patterns:** Avoid anything that reads as 'corporate' -- Material Design, Swiss International, and generic SaaS styling.

### Millennials (29-44)
- **Resonant Styles:** editorial-minimalism, bento-ui, dark-mode-ui, scandinavian-minimalism, analog-film-grain
- **Anti-Patterns:** Avoid both hyper-youth aesthetics (pixel art, Y2K) and over-formal luxury (Rococo, full Art Deco).

### Gen X / Boomers (45+)
- **Resonant Styles:** swiss-international, flat-design, material-design, studio-product, cinematic
- **Anti-Patterns:** Avoid low-contrast styles (neomorphism), chaotic layouts (deconstructivism), and motion-heavy patterns.
